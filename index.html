What is the fundamental difference between the data discussed in chapter 7 and the data discussed in chapter 8?

In Chapter 7 Data is used more as the numerical representation of statistical data. These pieces of data are also used to understand the Domain and Range, Max and Min, and Scales. While in chapter 8 Data is presented as the Visualization of the previously mentioned "Data" in chapter 7. Such as Axes or the Representation of the previous mentioned Max/Min, Domain/Range.

What are the pros and cons of 3 visualizations techniques discussed in chapter 8?

Pros

  1. Positioning Axes - Allows clear representation of where the data is being graphed and the Range of the data points presented.
  2. Time Based Axes - Shows acurate representation of when certain events happened/are happening.

Cons

  1. Positioning Axes - Being able to put the axis on top can mess up the over all appeal of your graph
  2. Coloring Axes - This could easily draw attention away from the physical data represented on the graph.
  3. Time Based Axes - Can really off put what the data is meant to represent.
